Flexible Diet

Here at WeightLoss we have a different view on the traditional and largely used Diet plans.

We believe that a Diet based on the lack of intake of certain aliments in order to try and lose weight is extremely harmful for your body. This is why we encourage you to take a new approach and try the FLEXIBLE DIET.

Flexible Diet is a diet that will help you lose weight while fitting around your lifestyle and preferences and it is only based on the control of 3 macros:

    - Carbohydrates
    - Protein
    - Fats

We encourage you to CHECK OUT this Flexible Diet website! It contains a lot more detailed information on this Diet and, combined with WeightLoss© we can garantee you will see huge improvements!
